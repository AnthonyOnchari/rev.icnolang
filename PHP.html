About Rev. I.C.N Olang

Rev. Isaac C.N. Olang is the founder and senior pastor of Active Word Apostolic Centre, a ministry dedicated to teaching and preaching the whole counsel of God without adding or subtracting from the Holy writ.

With a passion for restoring foundational apostolic doctrine and mending the cracks of Christian leadership, Rev. Olang focuses on equipping believers with proper understanding of God, the Church, the world, the devil, and who they are in Christ.

His ministry emphasizes the activation of gifts and talents of the Holy Spirit that lie latent due to ignorance, releasing mature believers into productive ministry as co-laborers with Christ.

Motto: BUILDING LIVING TEMPLES!

Mission & Vision

Vision

Win one, Build one, Release one

To see believers transformed into Christlike maturity and released into productive ministry as co-laborers with Christ.

Mission

  • Equip believers with proper understanding of God, the Church, the world, the devil and who they are in Christ
  • Train believers with an aim of adding workforce in the harvest field
  • Activate gifts and talents of the Holy Spirit lying latent
  • Release mature believers into productive ministry
  • Teach and preach the whole counsel of God
  • Restore foundational apostolic doctrine
  • Facilitate aggressive propagation of the great commission

Purpose Statement

Win people to Jesus, incorporate them into God's family, develop them to Christlike maturity, equip them for ministry and life mission in the world to glorify God.
